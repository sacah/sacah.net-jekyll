I passed the tests and my personality is inline with being a Student Paramedic.

Next step is Medical Assessment and Health Related Fitness Assessment (HRFA). I have 60 days to complete both these.

Medical Assessment contains 2 parts. Part 1 you fill out, asks about your medical history. Part 2 your local GP fills out.

HRFA is all the excersises you can read about in the preperation PDF on the QAS website, and must be done through an approved provider. There are 2 such providers on the Gold Coast, so I guess I'll be giving them a call shortly.
