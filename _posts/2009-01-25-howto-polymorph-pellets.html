'HowTo: Polymorph Pellets' date: '2009-01-25T19:08:00.004+10:00' author: sacah tags: - howto - project modified_time: '2009-01-31T17:24:05.867+10:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4605009901196846959.post-2985269779189555691 blogger_orig_url: http://www.sacah.net/2009/01/howto-polymorph-pellets.html --- I recently bought some polymorph plastic pellets from Jaycar. I hadn't seen these before, so I was quite excited when I saw them.

Many people report using hair dryers or heat guns, these require about 60degerees Celsius to become soft and allow you to mold them.

I simple put boiling water into a glass dish and added the number of pellets I needed. Once they turn clear you know it's time to pull them out and start molding them. I used a wooden skewer to group them all together and get them out of the hot water.

Leave at room temperature and they will turn white and become hard. They will then be drillable.
