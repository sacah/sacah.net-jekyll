--- layout: post title: JVC GZ-MG330 date: '2008-11-29T23:17:00.002+10:00' author: sacah tags: modified_time: '2008-11-29T23:28:56.663+10:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4605009901196846959.post-4221305572665553492 blogger_orig_url: http://www.sacah.net/2008/11/jvc-gz-mg330.html --- For our upcoming holiday we figured it was time to buy a digital video camera. After looking around I settles on a HDD version, and based on price and features settled on the JVC MG330. Got this from Ted's Cameras for $599AUD which included a JVC Camera bag and a second standard BN-VF808J battery.

As this is the first dedicated video camera I had owned I can't give an expert opinion, but my first impressions I am quite satisfied with it. Very easy to use, I like the laser touch idea to keep greasy mits off the screen. I have a (un)natural shake in my hands, so was a bit worried about it not having an Optical Image Stabiliser, but the digital stabiliser does a good job at optical zoom levels, which is 35x.

Feel free to ask any questions about this camera, I'll answer what I can to help anyone interested in it.
