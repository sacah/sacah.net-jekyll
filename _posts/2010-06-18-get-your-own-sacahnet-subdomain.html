--- layout: post title: Get your own sacah.net subdomain date: '2010-06-18T20:32:00.000+10:00' author: sacah tags: - dns modified_time: '2010-06-18T20:32:49.231+10:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4605009901196846959.post-2992562705238842543 blogger_orig_url: http://www.sacah.net/2010/06/get-your-own-sacahnet-subdomain.html --- So I've been using afraid.org for my free DNS for about 3 years, and as part of making their service free others can request to have subdomains from your domain.
So if for some weird reason you want a sacah.net subdomain, head over to afraid.org for dns for free, signup and once you login click 'Registry' in the left menu. Then search for sacah.net and request to add a subdomain, or look through the thousands of other domains on there and maybe you'll find one you like better (-:
