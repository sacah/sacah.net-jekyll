--- layout: post title: LED Grow Lights date: '2008-05-10T17:50:00.004+10:00' author: sacah tags: - project modified_time: '2009-07-25T14:14:28.938+10:00' thumbnail: http://bp3.blogger.com/_-gckRFXob4c/SCVVzBQwlVI/AAAAAAAAAAU/WJlcBycXbac/s72-c/led_side_pcb.png blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4605009901196846959.post-170173316386118950 blogger_orig_url: http://www.sacah.net/2008/05/led-grow-lights.html --- I have an interest in alternate methods of growing plants, when I read about people using LEDs to grow them I though it was a quick cheap way I could enter into this alternate methods and start experimenting.
I bought 10 Red, 2 Blue and 2 Yellow 10mm LEDS and soldered them onto some PCB. The Yellows are 3600mcd, the Blues are 4500mcd and the Reds are 4000mcd.
